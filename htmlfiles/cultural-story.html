Tribal Cultural Stories

Warli Art Tradition

Warli art is an ancient tribal painting style from Maharashtra, using simple shapes to show village life, festivals, and nature.

Dhokra Craft Legacy

Dhokra is a traditional metal craft using the lost-wax method, practiced by tribal communities for thousands of years.

Bamboo Craft Culture

Bamboo craft reflects tribal sustainability, creating baskets, tools, and household items using natural bamboo.
